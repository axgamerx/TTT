What is tictactoe?

TicTacToe is a simple children's game. The goal is to place 3 of your marker in a row and prevent your opponent from doing the same. Also, diagonals count!
America is one of the only contries that actually calls this game 'TicTacToe'. some of the other names are:

  • noughts and crosses
    • origin: United Kingdom
  • x's and o's
    • origin: Ireland
  • desas
    • meaning: sausage
    • origin: Latvia

Terminology

Fork
A situation where, no matter where the opponent goes, you win
Since we are assuming the opponent has at least a basic understanding of tictactoe, this is the only way to win.
